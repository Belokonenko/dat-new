Payment and delivery

Pay

The minimum order for seeds or any other goods in our store is 200 $.
  • To a current account at CB PrivatBank

    In any branch of the bank, you can make a payment to a current account either through the PrivatBank CB terminal or the Privat24 program. The cost of bank services (commission) is paid separately, according to the tariffs of the bank chosen by you.

  • Cash on delivery through "Ukrposhta Express" (payment upon receipt of the parcel)

    The order is paid upon receipt at the branch. The commission for transferring payment for an order worth up to UAH 500 is UAH 10, over UAH 500 - 2%. The commission will NOT be added to the account and is paid upon receipt of the package.

  • Cash on delivery through the company "Nova Poshta" (payment upon receipt of shipment)

    The order is paid upon receipt at the branch. Commission for money transfer - 2.5%, but not less than UAH 10 of the order amount. The commission is paid upon receipt of the parcel.

  • Cash on pickup.

    The order is paid in cash upon receipt at our office, located at Kramatorsk, 7 Bogdana Khmelnytskogo St., "Ogorodnyk" store (UkrNasinya).

When choosing to pay after payment, consider:

  • goods marked "MADE TO ORDER" and "PAYMENT IN ADVANCE" are not sent by this method;

  • until payment for the first order is received, the next order will not be sent to the client. The company reserves the right to change the price in case of exchange rate fluctuations. Ordering seeds, etc. of goods via the Internet is processed up to 3 days, during the season, due to an increase in the number of orders, the processing time may be increased.It is possible that some goods are not in stock, they are not included in the invoice, they are not replaced by others without agreement with the buyer. After processing and forming the seed order via the Internet, an invoice is sent to the e-mail specified by the client and to his office. If within 3 days after placing the order, the seeds have not arrived at your e-mail address account, please inform the operator. Please add our e-mail shop@ukrsemena.com to your address book, so the invoice will not end up in spam. The invoice will contain details for payment according to the selected method. The list of goods in the order status "Invoiced" in the client's account completely matches the invoice. The ordered product is reserved for 3 working days, during which the client must make payment (if prepayment is selected).

    All necessary payment details are indicated in the invoice.

    Advance payment is made only after receiving the invoice. The bill must be paid within 3 banking days. When making a payment, the commission is not included in the price of the order and delivery, it is paid separately by the buyer. If you are refused payment at a bank branch, contact the Contact Center and report the violation, specifying the name and position of the responsible bank employee.

After paying for the order, send a letter to shop@ukrsemena.com or contact the Contact Center and inform:
  • Name;
  • order number;
  • the amount paid;
  • payment date

This will speed up the dispatch of the order. If you are going to pay an overdue invoice, be sure to contact the manager to clarify the availability of the product.

Delivery

"Ukrposhta Express" delivery

Delivery of seeds by mail across Ukraine can be ordered both by post-payment and by pre-payment. The order is delivered to the post office indicated by the client. The maximum weight of the package is up to 10 kg. The duration of delivery of seeds by mail across Ukraine is no more than 5 days.

The cost of delivery is calculated according to the tariffs of "Ukrposhta Express", it is NOT included in the order and is paid upon receipt of the parcel.

WARNING! The period of free storage of parcels in PJSC "Ukrposhta" branches is 5 working days from the moment of arrival. Starting from the 6th day of storage, the parcel is returned to the sender. Please contact the offices of PJSC "Ukrposhta" in a timely manner to receive orders.

Delivery by commercial transport companies

The order is received at the company's branches. The customer pays for the delivery. Delivery to the client's address is possible for an additional fee. The duration of delivery by commercial transport services is no more than 3 days.

The cost of delivery is calculated according to the tariffs of transport companies, is NOT included in the order and is paid upon receipt of the parcel.

Pickup

The client can receive and pay for the parcel at our office at

  • Kramatorsk, str. B. Khmelnytskogo, 7, UkrNasinya online store (Ogorodnyk store).
  • Parcel delivery schedule: Monday - Friday from 9:00 a.m. to 6:00 p.m., break 12:30 p.m. to 1:30 p.m.

Orders are not sent to territories not controlled by Ukraine. We apologize!

After sending the order, the customer is notified by e-mail and by phone the number of the goods and transport invoice (declaration) and the estimated date of arrival. To receive the parcel, you must have a passport with you (if the client receives the parcel in person) or a power of attorney (if you represent the interests of the recipient). When ordering seeds by mail in an online store from Ukraine, a parcel with the order arrives at the client's post office. Employees of the post office/transport company must send him an alert and/or SMS message about the receipt of the parcel, so you need to monitor your postal box. Very often, messages are lost, so based on the approximate date of arrival of the parcel, inquire at the post office about the parcel yourself. It must be received within 5 days from the day of arrival, after which the parcel is returned to the online store.

If the client does not pick up the parcel from his post office on time, the UkrNasinya online store is not responsible for his inaction or the inaction of the post office employees and will not resend the parcel. Such customers are entered into the black list and from that moment on can receive the goods only after full prepayment.

This also applies to the delivery of seeds with cash on delivery by the "Nova Poshta" company.

When receiving an order with seeds, etc. goods, carefully inspect the parcel for the integrity of the packaging. In case of violation of the integrity of the packaging and/or missing part of the order and/or damage to the product, in the presence of an employee of the transport company or PJSC "Ukrposhta" conduct an inspection of the contents of the parcel, compare it with the enclosed delivery invoice for the goods, certified by the signature of an employee of the UkrNasinnia online store. If necessary, draw up an "Inspection Report" in which the condition of the packaging and goods, the nature of the damage, must be reflected. After drawing up the Act, the client must immediately contact the Contact Center of the UkrNasinya online store and provide a copy of the act on product inspection. If the employees of the delivery service refuse you to conduct an inspection and draw up the Act, the customer has the right not to sign the goods and transport documents and not to pick up the order.The presence of the consignee's signature in the accompanying invoice means that the goods have been received and claims against the seller and the company that delivered the seeds, etc. there are no products.
